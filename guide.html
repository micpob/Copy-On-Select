Welcome to Copy on Select!

How it works:

COPY

  • Just select some text and it will get copied automatically.
  • Hold down the Ctrl key (Command on Mac) while selecting if you want to bypass the auto-copying.
  • Everytime you make a new selection, you will see a short popup alert near your mouse pointer. You can deactivate it in the Options page.

PASTE

Method 1: using double click

  • Double click on a field while holding down the Ctrl key (Command on Mac) and the copied text will be automatically pasted.
  • Hold down the Shift key too and the field will be emptied of any pre-existing content before pasting.
  • You can deactivate any of this functionalities in the .

Method 2: using middle click

  • Do a middle click (mouse wheel) on a field while holding and the copied text will be automatically pasted.
  • Hold down the Shift key too and the field will be emptied of any pre-existing content before pasting.
  • Hold down the Ctrl key (Command on Mac) while middle clicking and the automatic pasting wiull be bypassed.
  • You can deactivate any of this functionalities in the Options page.

CAVEATS

  • The extension does not work on any page whose URL starts with "Chrome", for example chrome://extensions/ or chrome://settings/ (this is standard for any extension, for security reasons)
  • The extension does not work on the page that opens when you click on the "New Tab" plus sign in Chrome. Usually this is the google homepage (same as above, this is standard for all Chrome extensions)
  • The extensipn only work in the browser's URL bar (because it is outside of the browsing window)
